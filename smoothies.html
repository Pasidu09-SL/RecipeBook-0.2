Berry Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup strawberries
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1/2 cup raspberries
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy.
