Alfredo Pasta

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add heavy cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted.
  5. Toss the sauce with cooked pasta. Season with salt and pepper.
