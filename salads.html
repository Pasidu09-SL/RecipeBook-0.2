Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Chop the romaine lettuce and place in a large bowl.
  2. Add grated Parmesan cheese and croutons.
  3. In a small bowl, mix Caesar dressing with minced garlic and lemon juice.
  4. Toss the salad with the dressing until well coated.
