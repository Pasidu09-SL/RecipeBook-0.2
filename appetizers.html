Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 baguette

Instructions

  1. Chop the tomatoes and basil.
  2. Mix with minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Slice the baguette and toast the slices.
  4. Top the toasted baguette slices with the tomato mixture.
